The story

Started by friends.
Staffed by professionals.
Same people.

In 2018, a group of friends from tech, finance, music, nightlife, and entertainment kept ending up in charge of everyone else's fun. The sound system. The boat. The bartender who never showed. Somebody had to handle it, and it kept being us.

So we made it official. Frienders is an experiences company. We produce our own parties in NYC, across the US, and internationally. We host private events for people who want theirs handled the same way. And everything we use to do it, from the venue to the speakers to the staff behind the bar, is available to you.

The rule from day one has not changed: every event should feel like it was thrown by your most capable friend. Because it was.
